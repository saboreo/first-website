STORAGE

Do you have more garden stuff than space in which to store it? If the answer's yes, you need to check out our great range of garden storage boxes and cupboards.

For the avid gardener, these handy hideaways are ideal and provide you with the perfect place to put your gardening tools, fertiliser and anything else you might need to store safely.

If you're looking to keep your garden neat and tidy with some larger storage solutions, we have some fantastic garden sheds. With plenty of storage room available, they can easily be converted into a garden retreat for a little bit of outdoor luxury or even convert into a playhouse for the children.

If your budget is a little bit bigger, our conservatories range is the ideal way to add an extra garden room. To make your outdoor space even more relaxing, check out our selection of hot tubs and spas for a little touch of extravagance.

Please ask the staff in our garden centre for more information and advice about furnishing your garden.
